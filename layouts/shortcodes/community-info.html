Community Meetings

We have two recurring bi-weekly Harbor community meetings. One meeting will cater to our Asia and Europe members at 9PM UTC+8 time zone, and a second meeting will cater to our Americas members at 4PM Eastern / 1PM Pacific time zone. We will use these meetings to communicate updates to the project, listen to feedback from the Harbor community, and also collect requirements/use cases for new features.

Bi-weekly public community meetings

See all the meeting information and add our calendar over at our schedule to get the latest Harbor news, what we're working on, and provide suggestions and feedback. Read, comment, or add agenda items on the meeting notes.
