# Project Harbor at Open Source Summit India and KubeCon + CloudNativeCon India 2026

The Harbor community is excited to be part of both **Open Source Summit India 2026** and **KubeCon + CloudNativeCon India 2026** in Mumbai. If you are attending either event, this is a great opportunity to meet Harbor contributors, talk about the project roadmap, and learn how Harbor is evolving for modern cloud-native artifact management and software supply chain security.

Harbor continues to be a key part of the CNCF ecosystem, helping teams store, secure, replicate, and manage container images and OCI artifacts. Across Open Source Summit India and KubeCon India, we are looking forward to connecting with operators, platform engineers, security teams, and new contributors who are building with Harbor or exploring it for the first time.

## Open Source Summit India: LFX Mentorship Showcase

Harbor will be represented at the **LFX Mentorship Showcase** during Open Source Summit India with a poster session by [Lakshit](https://github.com/nucleofusion) on Harbor CLI mentorship work.

**When:** Tuesday, June 16, 2026, 7:10pm - 8:30pm IST  
**Where:** Solutions Showcase, Lotus 1, Third Floor

If you are interested in Harbor CLI, contributor mentorship, or getting involved in open source project work, this is a great opportunity to connect with the Harbor community and learn more about ongoing mentorship work.

## KubeCon + CloudNativeCon India: Visit Harbor at Booth T-12

Harbor will also be available during the full showcase hours on both days of KubeCon + CloudNativeCon India.

**Harbor booth:** T-12  
**When:** Thursday, June 18 and Friday, June 19, 2026  
**Showcase hours:** Full showcase hours

Stop by to:

- Meet Harbor maintainers, contributors, and community members
- Ask questions about running Harbor in production
- Learn about OCI artifact management, replication, vulnerability scanning, and policy workflows
- Discuss GitOps, edge use cases, and secure software delivery
- Find out how to get involved with the Harbor community

Whether you are already using Harbor or just getting started, we would love to hear what you are building and where the project can help.

## KubeCon + CloudNativeCon India: Lightning Talk

Harbor will also be featured in a project lightning talk at KubeCon India.

**Project Lightning Talk: Harbor Project Update: Gitless GitOps, Satellite at the Edge**  
**Speaker:** Prasanth Baskar, Subproject Maintainer  
**When:** Thursday, June 18, 2026, 12:07pm - 12:12pm IST  
**Where:** Lotus 1, Level 3  
**Session details:** [https://sched.co/2Imhr](https://sched.co/2Imhr)

This short session will highlight recent Harbor project updates and explore where the project is headed, including Gitless GitOps workflows and Harbor Satellite at the edge.

## Join the Harbor Community

Open Source Summit and KubeCon are two of the best places to meet the people behind open source and CNCF projects and talk directly with the community. If you are interested in artifact management, supply chain security, OCI, GitOps, edge deployments, or contributing to Harbor, please come say hello.

We will be at **booth T-12** throughout the full showcase hours on June 18 and 19, at the **LFX Mentorship Showcase** on Tuesday evening, and you can also catch the lightning talk on Thursday at **12:07pm IST** in **Lotus 1, Level 3**.

See you at Open Source Summit India and KubeCon + CloudNativeCon India 2026!
