# Project Harbor at KubeCon + CloudNativeCon Europe 2026 in Amsterdam

The cloud-native community is once again gathering for one of the most anticipated events of the year — **KubeCon + CloudNativeCon Europe 2026**, taking place in the beautiful and dynamic city of Amsterdam, Netherlands. As always, **Project Harbor** will be proudly represented, bringing the latest innovations in container registry technology, security, and software supply chain management to the global Kubernetes community.

KubeCon Europe is where maintainers, contributors, and users from across the ecosystem come together to collaborate, share knowledge, and shape the future of cloud-native technologies. Harbor continues to play a key role in this ecosystem, empowering organizations worldwide to securely manage and distribute their container artifacts.

# Harbor's Presence at KubeCon Europe 2026

The Harbor community is excited to connect with contributors, users, and new adopters throughout the event. From project updates to hands-on conversations in the Project Pavilion, there will be plenty of opportunities to learn more about Harbor’s growing ecosystem.

## Maintainers Track

The Harbor maintainers will present a **Maintainers Track session** where attendees can get a deeper look into the project’s evolution, roadmap, and ongoing development efforts.

**Harbor Project – Maintainers Session**

Join us to learn more about:

- Harbor architecture and ecosystem integrations
- Recent improvements and upcoming roadmap items
- Community growth and contribution opportunities
- Real-world adoption across the cloud-native ecosystem

## Lightning Talk

Harbor will also be featured in a **Lightning Talk**, offering a quick and engaging update on the project and its role within the CNCF ecosystem.

**Project Lightning Talk – Harbor**

Lightning talks are always a great opportunity to get concise insights into exciting developments happening across CNCF projects.

## Project Pavilion

## Growing the Harbor Ecosystem

Over the past year, Harbor has continued to expand its capabilities around **artifact management, security scanning, policy enforcement, and supply chain security**. The project remains deeply integrated with the broader CNCF ecosystem and continues to evolve alongside technologies like Kubernetes, OCI artifacts, and modern DevSecOps workflows.

Events like KubeCon play a vital role in strengthening these collaborations and ensuring Harbor continues to meet the needs of cloud-native practitioners worldwide.

## Join the Harbor Community

If you're attending **KubeCon + CloudNativeCon Europe 2026**, make sure to stop by the Harbor booth in the **Project Pavilion** and say hello. Whether you’re a long-time Harbor operator, a contributor, or simply curious about the project, we welcome you to connect with us.

The Harbor community thrives because of its users and contributors, and KubeCon is the perfect place to strengthen those connections.

## See You in Amsterdam!

The Harbor team is looking forward to another inspiring KubeCon filled with collaboration, learning, and community building. Amsterdam provides the perfect backdrop for bringing together the global cloud-native ecosystem.

If you’re passionate about **secure software supply chains, artifact management, and open-source collaboration**, Harbor would love to meet you there.

**See you at KubeCon Europe 2026 in Amsterdam!**
