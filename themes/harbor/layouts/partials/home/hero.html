What is Harbor?

Harbor is an open source cloud native registry that stores, signs, and scans container images for vulnerabilities.

As a private on-premises registry, Harbor solves common challenges by delivering trust, compliance, performance, and interoperability. It fills a gap for organizations and applications that cannot use a public or cloud-based registry, or want a consistent experience across clouds.
